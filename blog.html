About us

Telangana University was established through Act No 28 Of the state government in the year 2006. The University was established with an objective to make the Higher Education accessible to the educationally backward District of Nizamabad in Telangana. The University is the first among the newly formed Universities in the state. It is the third largest university next to Osmania University and Kakatiya University in Telangana state. It has the largest number of Courses(26) and also the First University to launch Ph.D Programme in a total of 13 Disciplines among all the newly formed universities in the state. The TU has three different and Spacious Campuses i.e., Main campus at Dichpally, South Campus at Bhiknoor and the third campus is at Sarangapur.
